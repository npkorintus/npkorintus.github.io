Bernie Sanders is a U.S. Senator representing the state of Vermont.

Quick timeline of Bernie Sanders' Life:

  • Born on September 8, 1941 in Brooklyn, NY
  • Graduates from the University of Chicago, B.A. in political science, 1964
  • Registered as an Independent
  • Elected as mayor of Burlington, VT in 1991, serves four terms from 1981-1989
  • Elected to U.S. House of Representatives in 1990, serves eight terms from 1991-2007
  • Co-founds the Congressional Progressive Caucus in 1991
  • Elected to U.S. Senate, serving from January 4, 2007 to the present
  • Ran for president in 2016
  • Currently running for president in 2020 election
